Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data.

Effective Date: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with our website, specifically by reaching out to us via our contact form or to book a demo, we collect the following personal information:

  • Your Name
  • Email Address
  • Organization / Company Name
  • Any additional information you choose to provide in your message

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide strictly for business communication. This includes responding to your inquiries, scheduling penetration testing assessments, and providing updates regarding ThreatForged AI services. All contact form submissions are securely processed and routed through our backend provider, Formspree.

3. No Sale or Sharing of Personal Data

ThreatForged AI operates entirely as a cybersecurity service provider. We value your privacy as much as your security. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third-party data brokers, marketers, or any external entities.

4. Trackers and Cookies

As a security company, we respect your digital footprint. Our website currently uses no advertising trackers, pixels, or invasive monitoring tools.

Cookie Policy: If we choose to implement basic, privacy-respecting analytics in the future to understand site performance, this policy will be updated to explicitly disclose that usage. We do not use cookies for cross-site tracking or targeted advertising.

5. Data Deletion and Your Rights

You have the right to know what data we hold about you and to request its immediate deletion. To request the removal of your personal information from our systems and contact records, please email ryan@threatforged.ai. We will process your request promptly and without penalty.

6. Texas Data Privacy and Security Act (TDPSA) Compliance

In compliance with the Texas Data Privacy and Security Act, we transparently disclose our data collection practices as outlined above. We do not engage in the sale of personal data or process personal data for targeted advertising. Texas residents retain the right to access, correct, delete, and obtain a copy of their personal data by contacting us at the email address provided above.
